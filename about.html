All About Us

We are dedicated to helping families make every moment count by getting connected and staying connected. One click at a time.

Our Vision

F.U.N's primary purpose is to bridge the gap between families, to provide a safe virtual space that any age can use easily, quickly, and effortlessly. We want to connect and keep families safe while social distancing no matter how close or far aprart they are during these unsure times. F.U.N.

Values

F.U.N. believes that family is what matter the most. You can rest assure that we are always thinking of ways to help support your family unit.

What We Are About

Family is important, make the most of every moment together near or far. With F.U.N. you don't ever have to feel the distance of your loved ones again. Communication and staying in touch is important now more than ever. COVID-19 has impacted all of our families in many differnet ways, and staying in touch in an easy seamless way is vital. The idea of feeling connected is very reinforcing, to all of us, and it contributes to our happiness and mental well being.

We Are All About Support

The importance of communication in families is strong and varied. F.U.N. is here to help support you and your family keep the connection going. No matter what your family dynamic needs are, we got you! With our talk to text feature, video chatting, game room, picture share, there is something for everyone. Good family communication is important because families are what we most often turn to for support. So let us help you support each other.

Virtual Visits
